What is Acting?

Acting can be thought of as the study of the human emotion of a character in a story. To be able to feel and express these emotions as a form of entertainment is the goal of acting.

History of Acting

Acting is not a recent concept that modern humans thought of. Ever since man walked the Earth, acting has always been practiced. For example, imitation can be thought of as a form of acting when early humans would imitate animals' characteristics. It wasn't until around the 1800s that acting became specialized. Acting Tips

Acting can be daunting for someone who is just starting out. Here are some recommended tips for those who are new to acting.

  • Sign up and attend a local acting class.
  • Speak out loud when reading lines.
  • Study into the history of the character you are portraying.
  • Practice, practice, practice!
